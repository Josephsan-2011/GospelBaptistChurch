Our Pastor

Rev. Dr. Pastor Paul

Rev. Dr. Pastor Paul serves as the Senior Pastor of Gospel Baptist Church, faithfully leading the congregation with a deep commitment to biblical preaching, discipleship, and community outreach. He is passionate about proclaiming the Word of God with clarity and conviction, equipping believers to grow in their faith and live out the Gospel in everyday life. He earned his degrees in Theology, Ministry, Biblical Studies, and Evangelism from Shalom Bible College in West Des Moines, IA. With more than 22 years of diverse experience in Christian ministry, Rev. Dr. Pastor Paul brings both wisdom and dedication to his calling. His ministry is marked by a heart for people, a vision for the church, and an unwavering commitment to glorifying God in all that he does.

Welcome Message

We are delighted to welcome you to Gospel Baptist Church. Our church is a place where people from all walks of life come together to worship, learn, and grow in their faith. Whether you're a long-time believer or just beginning your spiritual journey, you'll find a warm and welcoming community here.

Join us every Sunday and Saturday as we gather to praise God, study His Word, and encourage one another in our walk with Christ. We look forward to meeting you and sharing in the joy of serving our Lord together.
